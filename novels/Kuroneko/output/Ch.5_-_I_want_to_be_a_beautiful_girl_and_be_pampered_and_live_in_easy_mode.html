Ch. 5 - I just want to see some Teteee

I want to be a beautiful girl and be pampered and live in easy mode!

There are 6 members in the same generation as Kuroneko-san.

Since [Arutema] still only has a few members, the company's strategy was to make the debuts all at once during this period.

In a year from now, the debut rush will slow down a bit, and each debut will shift to three people at a time, in which each generation will be considered as a group.

Well, it's not that time yet.

So debuting at this time means that if you want to make friends, you have to take initiative.

"Kuroneko-san, let's do a collab!"

"Eh, ah, collab...?"

"Yes, a collab!"

It all started with a single message.

Yui Natsuname, one of my gen-mates, messaged me and threw a bombshell by saying, "Let's talk"

The amount of contact between me and Yui Natsunami is close to none, when all of us in the same generation had a voice match before our debut.

The only words we exchanged were something like "Thank you for your support."

There's no way we could skip 2.3 steps and suddenly collaborate.

"When I saw Kuroneko-san first stream, I thought we could be friends since I also have communication problems! So I want to do a collab with you and be friends!"

That's a lie.

There's no way, a person with real communication problems would suddenly send a chat saying, "Let's talk now."

Even if it's an anonymous relationship, I won't know what to do, and if it's someone I have to keep talking with in the future, I'll be cautious with what I say.

If you just send a hello message, I'll review it at least 5 times, write it, let it rest for an hour, and then revise it again.

I'm the person who took a week to finish greeting my seniors.

In the first place, there aren't't many otakus in the world who carelessly say, "Let's talk!"

The true enemy of someone with communication issues is a call where you can't see the other person face!

I don't know what kind of expression the other person is making, so I can't make a decision based on their voice because I'm afraid of saying something wrong.

But I can't say that because I belong to a company.

If I was an ordinary VTuber, the worse you can do is use the legendary treasure sword "Ah, I was sleeping", but me and the other person are VTubers as a job.

There's no way to avoid calling! I decided to agree to it, and that's how we got here.

When I heard the word "collab", I regretted not pretending to be a sleep.

My will is softer than tofu.

"Would you like to collab with me...?"

"Ugh"

Even if I can't see her face, I can still imagine her expression.

Moist upturned eyes, it's the so-called persuasion by tears from over the screen.

A woman who can easily imagine someone crying face has communication problems? ...this joke went too far.

"What are we going to do in this collab...?"

"I'll ask the marshmallows for a theme and have a chatting stream!"

"Ch, chatting stream..."

Of all things, a chatting stream?

A chatting stream is that thing where you talk for an hour or two, right?

I also did chatting streams several times after the first stream, but it was different because the other person didn't speak.

Talking to a real person for more than an hour... just thinking about it made me feel nauseous.

"Why don't we do a gaming stream?"

In the case of a gaming stream, in the worst case the collab can happen even if I don't talk (not possible)

The viewers will surely laugh and forgive me if I say, "I got too into it☆"

Fortunately, the streaming equipment arrived yesterday, so gaming collabs are always welcome.

However.

"Chatting stream! Would you like to have a chatting stream with me?"

"Ugh... let me think about it."

What the hell is wrong with this woman!

Are you doing this knowing I have a communication problem!

I'm guessing she's not the type of communication disorder that doesn't know how to talk, but the kind that can't read the atmosphere due to the habit of talking too much!

And without giving in to my groans the entire time, Yui Natsumi (forcibly) made me promise to collaborate with her three days later and ended the call.
