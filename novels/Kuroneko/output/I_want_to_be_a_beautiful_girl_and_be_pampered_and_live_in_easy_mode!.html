I want to be a beautiful girl and be pampered and live in easy mode!

Author: Akaba Renga, 紅葉煉瓦

Translator: Aurora

Raws: https://ncode.syosetu.com/n3156gc/

Synopsis:

A story about a transcendent beautiful girl who made her debut as a second generation member of the industry's first VTuber group "Arutema" and shines like the sun while burning.

In a dream, Kurone was really reincarnated when he asked Santa, "I want to be a beautiful girl and be pampered and live my life in easy mode!"

Her wish comes true, and she begins to lead a life as a beautiful girl, but despite her long-awaited reincarnation, she ends up spending days when nothing goes well due to her innate communication problems and laziness.

Before she knew it, she had a communication problem that was getting worse, and she felt that it would not be in easy mode.
